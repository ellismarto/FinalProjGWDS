Below are my 8 typographic system posters


Hover your mouse over the poster's to see description

Axial

All elements are organized either to the left or right of a single axis.

Radial

All elements are organized to extend from a central point of focus.

Random

All elements are organised randomly without definite aim, pattern, direction, rule, method or purpose.

Dilatational

All elements dilate from a circle or expand from a central point.

Grid

All elements are a system of vertical and horizontal divisions sthat organize and create relationships between elements.

Modular

All elements are placed in repeating structures to break up the content, the sections follow no specific pattern.

Bilateral

All elements are placed symmetrically centered on the axis.

Transitional

All elements are layered in an informal manner on top of each other.
